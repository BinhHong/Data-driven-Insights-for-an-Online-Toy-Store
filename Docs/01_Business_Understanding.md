
# Business Overview

Based on the available dataset, the company appears to be an online toy retailer selling products directly through its website. Revenue is generated through online product sales. The dataset captures the customer journey from website visit to refund.

# Business Model

Traffic -> Website Sessions -> Pageviews -> Orders -> Order Items -> Refunds

# Stakeholders

- Executive Management
- Marketing Manager
- Product Manager
- Finance

# Business Questions

- Is revenue growing?
- Which traffic source converts best?
- Which products generate the most revenue?
- Which products have the highest refund rate?
- How much revenue is lost through refunds?

# KPIs

- Revenue
- Orders
- Sessions
- Conversion Rate
- Average Order Value
- Refund Rate
- Net Revenue

# Dataset Limitations

- No geography
- No customer demographics
- No marketing spend
- No inventory
- No shipping costs

# Assumptions

- Sessions represent customer acquisition.
- Refunds reduce realized revenue.
- Products are sold directly through the website.

# Project Objective

The objective of this project is to identify the key drivers of traffic, conversion, revenue, and refunds across the customer journey and present actionable insights through an interactive Power BI dashboard.
